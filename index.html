Admission in the Canton of Zurich

How entry to a Zurich Gymnasium works

In the Canton of Zurich, entry to a Gymnasium normally requires passing the Zentralen Aufnahmeprüfung (ZAP), which takes place once a year at the beginning of March.

Zentrale Aufnahmeprüfung (ZAP)

The ZAP is the central entrance exam for learners who want to attend a Gymnasium in the Canton of Zurich.

Orientation events are announced directly on the websites of the cantonal schools.

Langgymnasium

Starts after Year 6 of primary school and lasts 6 years.

Kurzgymnasium

Starts after Year 2 or 3 of secondary school, or after Year 2 of Langgymnasium, and lasts 4 years.

Untergymnasium

The first two years of Langgymnasium are called Untergymnasium. In most cases, learners then transfer to the upper level without another exam.
